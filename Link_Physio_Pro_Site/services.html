Comprehensive PT

Treatment is guided by extensive physical therapy expertise, providing a personalized approach that includes education on pain management, movement mechanics, and exercises to support recovery and prevent future injury. Clients gain a deeper understanding of their body and how to optimize daily activities for long-term health.

Dry Needling

Utilizes small microfilament needles to release muscle tension, reduce pain, and improve mobility. By targeting trigger points and areas of muscle tightness, this technique helps accelerate recovery and enhance movement patterns.

Graston/IASTM

Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization (IASTM) and Graston techniques break down fascial restrictions and soft tissue dysfunction for lasting relief and improved movement. Highly effecitve in treating soft tissue injuries and strains.
