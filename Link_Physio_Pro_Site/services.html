Comprehensive Physical Therapy

Treatment is guided by extensive physical therapy expertise, providing a personalized approach that includes education on pain management, movement mechanics, and exercises to support recovery and prevent future injury. Clients gain a deeper understanding of their body and how to optimize daily activities for long-term health.

Dry Needling

Dry needling uses small, mico-filament needles inserted into muscular trigger points to reduce pain, decrease inflammation, and improve mobility—helping you move better and feel better.

IASTM / Graston Technique

Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization (IASTM) is a hands-on technique using specialized tools to reduce muscle tension, break down adhesions, relieve myofascial pain, and restore mobility.Effective for muscle strains and tendonitis

What to expect at Link Physio

Every visit begins with a comprehensive Initial Evaluation, where we review your medical history, movement goals, and any current limitations. From there, we conduct a detailed Assessment of your strength, mobility, and pain patterns to identify the root cause of your symptoms. We’ll then walk you through a personalized Discussion & Plan—a tailored strategy focused on relieving pain and restoring function. Your Treatment & Follow-Up sessions may include hands-on techniques designed to reduce pain, release tension, improve tissue mobility, and get you back to moving confidently. You'll also receive clear education and ongoing progress tracking to support long-term results.
