Comprehensive PT

Individualized evaluation and treatment for pain, injury, or movement dysfunction. Sessions include manual therapy, exercise prescription, and education. Aimed to help you return to pain free daily activity, exercise, sports, and general recreation.

Dry Needling

Releases tension and improves mobility by targeting muscle trigger points with fine, sterile needles. Effective for pain relief and restoring function.

Graston/IASTM

Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization (IASTM) and Graston techniques break down fascial restrictions and soft tissue dysfunction for lasting relief and improved movement.

Common Treatment Conditions

  • Low Back & Neck Pain: From acute strain to chronic discomfort, we target the root cause and restore confident movement.
  • Muscle Strains & Overuse Injuries: Whether from sports or daily activity, we help speed recovery and reduce risk of recurrence.
  • Rotator Cuff Dysfunction: Shoulder pain, weakness, or impingement — treated with a balance of manual therapy and progressive exercise.
  • Plantar Fasciitis & Heel Pain: Relieve stubborn foot pain and improve foot and ankle mechanics.
  • Tendonitis & Tendinopathy: Including Achilles tendonitis, patellar tendon issues, or tennis/golfer’s elbow.
  • Headaches & Migraines (Cervicogenic): Address muscle tension, joint stiffness, and posture that may be contributing to head pain.
  • Sports Injuries: From running, swimming, golfing, team sports, and more, we tailor treatment to your sport and goals.
