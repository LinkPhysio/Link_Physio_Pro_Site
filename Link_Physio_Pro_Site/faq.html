Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a physician referral?

Nope! In Florida, you can start physical therapy without a referral. Just reach out to Link Physio and we can get you booked for your first session.

Do you take insurance?

We keep things simple—Link Physio is completely cash-based. That means no surprise bills, no insurance restrictions, and no complicated paperwork. You pay at the time of your visit and know exactly what you’re getting.

Where do sessions take place?

All visits are by appointment only at Ripple Co-Working in Daytona Beach. It’s a comfortable, private space.

What happens at my first visit?

We’ll start with a conversation about your goals, history, and current concerns, followed by a full assessment. From there, we’ll jump right into your first treatment so you leave already feeling progress. Wear something comfortable and bring along any medical info you think might help.

What conditions do you treat?

From low back and neck pain to sports injuries, headaches, tendonitis, and vertigo, we treat a wide range of aches, pains, and movement issues. You can check out the Services page for the full list.

How do I schedule?

Easy—call, email, or use the Contact page to request your appointment. If you're not sure if it's a right fit, still feel free to reach out and we can discuss!
